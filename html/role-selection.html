Chapter 06
Role Selection
Match your natural instincts to the right role. Playing the wrong role is one of the most underrated reasons players stall at the same rank.
The 5 Roles
What Each Role Demands
🥇
Gold Laner
Micro

Farm safely, hit your power spike, carry teamfights. Positioning and kite mechanics are key.

Layla, Miya, Bruno, Beatrix, Granger, Karrie
⚔️
EXP Laner
Micro Lean

Hold your lane in 1v1 duels. Trading mechanics and wave management are essential.

Fredrinn, Esmeralda, Ruby, Zilong, Masha, Sun
🌿
Jungler
Micro

Control the whole map through ganks and objective pressure. Route efficiency is everything.

Hayabusa, Fanny, Ling, Lancelot, Karina, Aldous
🛡️
Roamer
Macro

Enable teammates and control teamfights. Team-first mindset is non-negotiable here.

Tigreal, Akai, Atlas, Khufra, Franco, Mathilda
🔮
Mid Laner
Both

Rotate and influence both sides of the map early. Hero choice dictates your playstyle.

Kagura, Yve, Harith, Chang'e, Pharsa, Lylia
Most Common Role Mistakes
What Each Role Gets Wrong
🥇Gold Laner: Rotating before your power spike — you're giving the enemy gold laner free farm. You NEED that farm. Rotate AFTER hitting your item spike.
⚔️EXP Laner: Dying 1v2 trying to hold lane vs jungler AND enemy laner. Know when to back off. You don't have to win — just don't feed two kills.
🌿Jungler: Sitting in jungle for 8 minutes farming. Your job is MAP IMPACT — clear and go. A jungler who doesn't gank plays 4v5 for the whole team.
🛡️Roamer: Buying damage items. Your HP and CC save more games than your kill count ever will. Roamers who build damage are roamers who feed. Period.
🔮Mid Laner: Still farming mid at 5 minutes. If you haven't influenced another lane by then, you're already losing the game for your team.
