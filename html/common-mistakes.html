B7
Common Mistakes
Fix the habits that silently throw games: waves, positioning, greed, and tempo.

1) Over-staying for 1 more wave

Leaving on time is a skill. Reset when you have a buy, when you’re low, or when the map goes dark.

2) Fighting without information

If you can’t see key enemies, assume they’re near you. Trade objectives or reset vision first.

3) Greeding jungle camps on the wrong timer

Take camps when it doesn’t cost waves, turret plates, or objective tempo.

4) Bad recalls

Recall when your next action is stronger after shopping, not when you’re already forced to.
