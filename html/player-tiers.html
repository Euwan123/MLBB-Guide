Golden Rules
7 Rules That Matter More Than Any Build
Rule 01
Win rate > Star count. A 60% WR player at Mythic always climbs. 48% WR is always stuck.
Rule 02
3 losses in a row = STOP. Come back tomorrow. Forced sessions bleed stars consistently.
Rule 03
Master 2–3 heroes DEEPLY before expanding. Jack of all trades = stuck in the same rank.
Rule 04
Your rank reflects your AVERAGE — not your best game. Consistency beats occasional brilliance.
Rule 05
If you can't explain WHY you lost, you haven't learned. Review one key moment per loss.
Rule 06
Ranked is a marathon. Consistent daily sessions beat 10-hour weekend grinds every season.
Rule 07
Upgrade your weakest skill — not your strongest. Your rank ceiling IS your biggest weakness.
