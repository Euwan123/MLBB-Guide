Power Spikes
Win games by forcing fights when you’re strongest, not when you feel like fighting.

3 types of power spikes

  • Level spike: hit ult levels first, then look for an immediate play.
  • Item spike: first core item or 2-item timing where your hero jumps in damage/utility.
  • Tempo spike: you shoved wave first and can move first.

How to play spikes

  • Ping before you move: if you rotate silently, team arrives late.
  • Convert kills: kill → objective → reset. Don’t chase forever.
  • Protect your spike: don’t throw it by taking a low-info fight.
