Common Sense
Simple rules that prevent 80% of throws.

Rules

  • If you can’t see them: they are coming to you.
  • Don’t fight on no ult: trade waves, reset, or take vision.
  • Objectives win games: kills are only a tool to take them.
  • Push then move: tempo starts with the wave.
  • Stop at 3 losses: tilt is expensive.
