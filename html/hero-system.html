Hero System
Build a real pool: roles, archetypes, and why you should stop random-picking.

Hero pool formula

  • 1 comfort main: your best carry.
  • 1 safe blind pick: consistent even when draft is bad.
  • 1 counter pick: punishes common meta heroes.

Archetypes

  • Engage: starts fights and creates picks.
  • Peel: protects carry and denies dives.
  • DPS: melts objectives and front line.
  • Burst: deletes key targets quickly.
  • Split: wins by pressure and trading.
