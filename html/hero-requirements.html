Hero Requirements
Minimum hero mastery benchmarks per tier. Hit these before pushing rank or you're setting yourself up for star loss.
Hero Difficulty
Easy · Medium · Hard — What They Mean
🟢 Easy Heroes
Simple mechanics, low skill floor
Consistent carry potential for beginners
Safe picks in almost any situation
Examples: Layla, Miya, Tigreal, Estes, Balmond, Zilong, Bruno, Akai
🟡 Medium Heroes
Moderate skill — requires map awareness
Positioning and timing matter heavily
Strong but punishing when played wrong
Examples: Chou, Lancelot, Kagura, Claude, Atlas, Pharsa, Fredrinn
🔴 Hard Heroes
High skill ceiling, demands precision
Mechanical mastery + game sense required
Highest reward when truly mastered
Examples: Fanny, Ling, Gusion, Hayabusa, Luo Yi, Selena
