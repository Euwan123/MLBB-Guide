Chapter 08
Laning
Lane control is the foundation everything else is built on. Win your lane consistently and ranking up becomes significantly easier.
Core Concepts
What Every Role Must Know
🌊
Wave Management
  • ↗ Slow Push: Let wave build big then crash — pressure while you roam
  • ↗ Fast Push: Clear instantly then roam or contest objectives
  • ↗ Freeze: Keep wave near your tower to deny enemy farm safely
  • ↗ Never push wave without a plan for what comes after
⚔️
Trading & Dueling
  • ↗ Only trade when you have HP, skill, or level advantage
  • ↗ Know your power spikes — trade aggressively only after hitting them
  • ↗ Bait out enemy defensive skills THEN go for the kill combo
  • ↗ Never trade near enemy tower — turret damage adds up fast
🗺️
Map Awareness
  • ↗ Check the minimap every 5–8 seconds minimum. No exceptions.
  • ↗ If jungler is missing from map, push less aggressively immediately
  • ↗ Track enemy's last seen position — predict where they rotated
  • ↗ Ping MIA immediately when your lane enemy disappears
🏗️
Tower Mechanics
  • ↗ Let minions tank tower shots — never walk under tower alone
  • ↗ Siege towers as a group after securing turtle/lord
  • ↗ Tower plates give gold — don't let them be taken for free early
  • ↗ Deny enemy from last-hitting your tower by contesting range
💰
Farm Efficiency
  • ↗ Last-hit minions whenever possible — gold difference compounds fast
  • ↗ Never stay in lane at full HP/mana doing nothing — always act
  • ↗ Know your item power spike — back right before or after it hits
  • ↗ Deny enemy jungle camps when winning lane to snowball
🔄
Rotation Timing
  • ↗ Rotate ONLY when your wave is pushed or cleared already
  • ↗ Ping intention 5–10 seconds before arriving so teammates wait
  • ↗ Don't rotate for fights your team is already winning — stay in lane
  • ↗ After a rotation, immediately return to lane or take an objective
Advanced Concepts
What Separates Good Laners from Great Laners
💡Level 4 Spike: Most heroes gain a massive power spike when their ultimate unlocks. This is your first window to trade aggressively — use it before the enemy reaches level 4 too.
💡Respawn Timing: When your laner dies, immediately push hard, take tower plates, or rotate. The 15–30 seconds they're dead is a window you must exploit every single time.
💡Vision Control: Clear enemy bush wards early. Laning blind is one of the leading causes of getting ganked over and over in the same spot.
💡Mental Laning: Sometimes winning lane doesn't mean kills — it means denying 30+ minions of farm. A 0-0 laner who denied 500 gold is winning silently and effectively.
