Content Creators
Watch with intent: one hero, one concept, then copy in game.

How to use creators correctly

  • Pause on decisions: ask “why here?”
  • Copy one thing per session: not everything at once.
  • Track results: keep what works, discard what doesn’t.

What to look for

  • Wave control: when they push and why.
  • Rotations: how early they arrive to objectives.
  • Item timing: what they build to solve the matchup.
