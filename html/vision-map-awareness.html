Vision & Map Awareness
Win by seeing the play before it happens: track enemies, timers, and fog threats.

Track 3 things

  • Missing lanes: if a laner disappears, expect a collapse.
  • Objective timer: move early to set space, not late to react.
  • Threat angle: where can they flank from?

Simple habit

Glance at the minimap every few seconds, especially after last hitting or clearing a camp.
