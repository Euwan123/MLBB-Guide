Rotation System
Rotate for tempo: push first, move first, and arrive first to objectives.

Rule: Push → Move

Clear your wave before roaming. A pushed wave buys time and forces answers.

Rule: Move on timers

Rotate before turtles/lord, before buffs, and before enemy spikes. Be early, not reactive.

Rule: Trade, don’t chase

If a fight is late or messy, take tower damage, jungle, or vision instead of forcing.
