Chapter 09
Banning
Most players ban their most hated hero without strategy. Smart banning removes the enemy's strongest win condition — not just the hero that annoyed you last game.
Ban Priority
What to Ban and Why
🚫 Always Ban
🔴 Current patch OP pick — whatever is statistically broken this update
🔴 Heroes you have ZERO counterplay knowledge against
🔴 Heroes that hard-counter your best hero or role
🔴 Heroes your team clearly cannot deal with in teamfights
⚠️ Situational
🟠 Enemy player's known main — check their profile first
🟠 Heroes that counter your entire team composition
🟠 Strong early heroes if your team scales late
🟠 Split-push heroes if your team has poor rotation
✅ Never Waste On
🟢 Heroes your team can handle with good positioning
🟢 Heroes that are weak in the current patch
🟢 Heroes you personally dislike but don't threaten your comp
🟢 Heroes you ban out of habit without thinking about draft
👑 High Rank Priority
⚡ Fanny — near-unkillable in the hands of a main
⚡ Mathilda — mobility + roaming power breaks games
⚡ Ling — map control and evasion hard to deal with
⚡ Atlas — one ultimate flip ends a teamfight entirely
Ban by Playstyle
Micro vs Macro — What to Prioritize
🗡️ MICRO Player Bans
Ban hard CC that interrupts your combo windows — Kaja, Diggie, Chou
Ban durable tanks that absorb your burst — Esmeralda, Uranus
Ban shields — Lolita and Estes completely negate your damage output
Ban anti-mobility heroes if you rely on dashes — Khufra is the biggest offender
Ban Diggie if your comp relies on CC chains — his passive denies every key ult
🛡️ MACRO Player Bans
Ban split-pushers that break your teamfight focus — Zilong, Sun, Aldous late game
Ban anti-initiation heroes that counter your teamfight setup — Diggie
Ban heroes that scale too hard and outpace your strategy — Claude, Granger
Ban poke mages if your team is melee-heavy — Pharsa and Chang'e kite forever
Ban strong duelists in EXP if your laner is macro-focused and not a duelist
💡 Mr. Yoo's Banning Rule
Always check the enemy's recent match history before locking in your ban. If one player has 30+ games on Fanny with 65%+ WR — that's your ban. Your feelings about what's "unfair" don't matter as much as removing the enemy's highest-probability win condition. Banning is pre-game strategy. Treat it like one.
