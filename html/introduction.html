📖 Introduction

Before diving into advanced strategies, understand the foundation. This guide is built on real experience: 8,000+ matches, 3 tournament wins, and countless pro player analysis.

What This Guide Covers

This isn't a hero guide or a tier list. It's about the patterns of thinking that separate players who climb from those who plateau. Every chapter builds on one fundamental truth: **the game is about reading situations, not reactions**.

Who This Is For

  • Players stuck at their current rank despite grinding
  • People who want to understand their mistakes instead of just losing
  • Solo/Duo queue players focused on climbing efficiently
  • Anyone who wants to think like a pro player

How to Use This Guide

Read in order. Each chapter builds on the previous one. Don't skip around. The "Dark System" tool at the beginning is a self-assessment — use it honestly to see if you're underperforming for your match count.

This guide is opinionated. Mr. Yoo doesn't teach "play safely every game" or "group up always." The meta changes. Strategies fail. But the underlying principles? Those work at ANY rank, in ANY meta.

Key Principles

  • Intention over grinding: 100 intentional matches beat 1,000 mindless ones
  • Patterns repeat: Learn to see them before your opponent does
  • Your mistakes, not your team: Control what you control
  • Solo queue is 1v5 win condition: Your 5 enemies are chaos. Your team is secondary

What Success Looks Like

After this guide, you should:

  • Understand WHY you lose, not just that you lose
  • Know your natural playstyle and how to exploit it
  • See win conditions 2 minutes before your enemy does
  • Play with intention every single game
  • Climb at least 1 rank per season, possibly more
