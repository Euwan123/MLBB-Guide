FAQ
Fast answers to common ranked problems.

Why do I lose even when I play well?

Most losses are macro losses: bad resets, bad wave states, late rotations, and low-info fights.

How do I climb faster?

  • One role: stop swapping every game.
  • Three heroes: comfort main, safe blind, counter.
  • Short sessions: quit when you’re tilted.

What should I practice first?

Wave timing, minimap awareness, and objective setup. Mechanics matter, but these decide most games.
