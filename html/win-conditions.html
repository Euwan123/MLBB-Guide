Win Conditions
Stop playing “fight everything”. Identify your win path and force the map into it.

Common win paths

  • Pick: catch one target then take objective.
  • Split: trade side pressure while team holds mid.
  • Front-to-back: protect carry, burn tank line, secure lord.

Rule

If your comp spikes at 2 items, don’t force 1-item fights. Stall, farm, and trade safely.
