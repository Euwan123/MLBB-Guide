💡 Pro Tips

  • MVP Ratio is Everything: If you have 2,000 matches but only 100 MVPs, that's a 1:20 ratio. You need at least 1:10.
  • Win Rate Matters More Than Matches: 1,000 matches at 60% WR beats 3,000 matches at 49% WR.
  • One Tricking Works: Most high-rank players dominate 2–3 heroes, not 50 different ones.
  • Watch Pros Play Your Main: Observe how they farm, rotate, and teamfight with your hero.
