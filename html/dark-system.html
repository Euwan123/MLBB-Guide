🌑 Are You a Dark System?

Discover if your performance matches your experience level. A "dark system" is a player who underperforms significantly for their match count.

What is a Dark System?

A dark system player has a match count that suggests higher competency than their actual results show. They grind matches but don't climb as expected.

Standards by Match Count

1,000 matchesExpected: 55%+ WRExpected: 100+ MVPs
2,000 matchesExpected: 54%+ WRExpected: 200+ MVPs
3,000 matchesExpected: 53%+ WRExpected: 300+ MVPs
4,000 matchesExpected: 52%+ WRExpected: 400+ MVPs
5,000+ matchesExpected: 53%+ WRExpected: 500+ MVPs

MVP Ratio

Your MVP ratio should be approximately 1 MVP per 10 matches (10:1 ratio). If you're below 1 per 15 matches, you're not delivering impact consistently.

How to Stop Being Dark System

  • Focus on consistency over grinding matches
  • Study your MVP games — what did you do differently?
  • Practice role-specific fundamentals
  • Play with intention, not just to reach a match count
  • Track only solo/duo queue for accurate self-assessment
