Chapter 01
Macro vs Micro
Before grinding ranked, understand your natural playstyle. Most players force a style that doesn't match them — and wonder why they're stuck for 5 seasons.
The Two Playstyles
🗡️
Micro Player
The Solo Carry — Individual Skill & Mechanics

Dominates through raw mechanics, precision combos, and solo outplays. Thrives in 1v1 situations, split pushing, and making game-winning plays entirely alone.

🏅 KELRA (ONIC PH/ID) — solo kills, split-push king
  • PROCan single-handedly carry even when team is far behind
  • PROHigh kill potential — punishes every enemy mistake hard
  • PROHard to stop when mechanically rolling and fed
  • CONOne big mistake can cost the entire game for everyone
  • CONVery prone to overextending and dying alone
  • CONStruggles when enemies draft specifically against you
VS
🛡️
Macro Player
The Strategist — Game Knowledge & Coordination

Dominates through game sense, map awareness, objective timing, and team coordination. Excels at teamfights, rotations, and lord/turtle control.

🏅 OHEB (Team Liquid PH) — teamfight, coordination king
  • PROWins through strategy even without raw mechanical edge
  • PROSafer — individual mistakes are less punishing overall
  • PROExcels at objective control and comeback mechanics
  • CONCan struggle in solo 1v1 lane duels against duelists
  • CONDependent on teammates actually following up
  • CONHarder to carry when teammates are consistently inting
⚠️ Important — Read This Before Judging Yourself
Being a Micro player does NOT make you bad at Macro — and vice versa. These are your natural strengths, not your ceiling. Master your dominant style first, then build the other side gradually. The best MLBB pro players are strong in BOTH.

Indicator 2 — In-Game Attitude
🗡️ You're MICRO if...
⚡ You WANT kills — always hunting for picks every second
⚡ You poke and bait enemies aggressively in lane
⚡ You go for 1v1 man-fights every chance you get
⚡ Playing passive makes you feel bored and useless
⚡ You're greedy — you can't help going for that extra kill
🛡️ You're MACRO if...
🎯 You PREFER objectives — lord, turtle, towers over kills
🎯 You freeze minions, deny farm, play safe in lane
🎯 You rotate to help teammates and set up their kills
🎯 Solo-diving alone feels too risky and wasteful
🎯 You're patient — you wait for the exact right moment
Mastery Tips
🗡️ Mastering MICRO
Channel your greediness RIGHT — master mechanics instead of fighting your instincts.
Perfect your hero's combo timing. Muscle memory = fewer deaths on outplays.
Learn enemy skill ranges — knowing when NOT to trade is peak micro awareness.
Only trade when you have HP, skill, or level advantage. Never trade out of ego.
Play 1 hero until mechanics are flawless THEN expand your hero pool.
Watch Kelra & Kairi — study HOW they decide to fight, not just that they fight.
Always have an escape plan before engaging. No exit route = death.
🛡️ Mastering MACRO
Study the minimap constantly — track enemies by their last seen position.
Master objective timing: Turtle at 4 min, Lord at 10 min — plan fights around this.
Ping before rotating. One ping saves your team from dying while you travel.
Learn the Lord Dance — bait enemies into a bad position BEFORE taking lord.
Don't push waves unless you plan to rotate or roam immediately after.
Follow up on teammates instantly — 5x strength when acting as one unit.
Track enemy jungler buff timers — predicting ganks = massive vision advantage.
In teamfights, staying ALIVE matters more than getting kills. Presence > KDA.
